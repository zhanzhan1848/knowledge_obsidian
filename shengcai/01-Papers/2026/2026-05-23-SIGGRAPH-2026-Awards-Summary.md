## Best Papers

### 1. GimmBO: Interactive Generative Image Model Merging via Bayesian Optimization
- **作者**: Chenxi Liu, Selena Ling, Alec Jacobson
- **领域**: 生成图像，贝叶斯优化

### 2. Mixwell: Sharp 2D Fluid Brushes for Progressive Physics-Based Mixing
- **作者**: Doug James, Ethan James
- **领域**: 流体模拟，GPU 加速

### 3. Walk on Decomposed Subdomains: Hybrid MC-Deterministic Solver for Elliptic PDEs
- **作者**: Clément Jambon, Mohammad Sina Nabizadeh, Mina Konaković Luković
- **领域**: 蒙特卡洛方法，PDE 求解

### 4. Robust Planar Maps for 3D Vectorization
- **作者**: Robert Fuchs, Keenan Crane
- **领域**: 3D 向量化，几何处理

### 5. Inverse Rendering for Discrete X-Ray Computed Tomography
- **作者**: Lovro Nuic, Ziyi Zhang, Korbinian Sager, Wenzel Jakob
- **领域**: 逆渲染，离散断层扫描
- **关键**: 基于梯度的离散层析成像方法，建模为材料概率分布，支持散射

## Honorable Mentions (渲染相关)

### Spatio-Temporal Control Variates with ReSTIR for Real-Time Rendering
- **作者**: Zhong Shi, Cunhao Wu, Lifan Wu, Kun Xu
- **领域**: 实时渲染，ReSTIR，控制变量

### Photons × Force: Differentiable Radiation Pressure Modeling
- **作者**: Charles Constant, Santosh Bhattarai, Elizabeth Bates, Marek Ziebart, Tobias Ritschel
- **领域**: 可微分渲染，辐射压力

### Sample Matching for Joint Extinction Gradient Estimation in Differentiable Volume Rendering
- **作者**: Ruihan Yu, Yu-Chen Wang, Jingwang Ling, Feng Xu, Shuang Zhao
- **领域**: 可微分体积渲染，梯度估计

### 2015: Convolutional Wasserstein Distances
- **作者**: Justin Solomon, Fernando de Goes, et al.

### 2014: Intrinsic Images in the Wild

### 2015: A Machine Learning Approach for Filtering Monte Carlo Noise
