# APU-Accelerated Large Eddy Simulation with the Discontinuous Galerkin Solver GALÆXI

## 论文信息
- **arXiv**: [2606.18927](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18927)
- **作者**: Spencer Starr et al.
- **日期**: 2026-06-17
- **分类**: physics.flu-dyn

## 核心贡献
异构GPU计算时代下GALÆXI DGSEM框架的优化与应用，AMD MI300A APU上的大规模验证。

## 背景
- **E级计算时代**: 异构GPU架构主导
- **DGSEM优势**: High-order accuracy + local computational stencil
- **挑战**: 传统CFD求解器需重新设计

## 数值方法
### GALÆXI框架
- **离散化**: Discontinuous Galerkin Spectral Element Method (DGSEM)
- **精度**: Up to 6th order accurate in interior
- **硬件**: AMD MI300A Accelerated Processing Units (APUs) @ Hunter supercomputer

### Wall-Modeled LES
- 集成wall-modeled LES算法到GPU加速框架
- 算法验证: Plane turbulent channel test case

## 验证案例
1. **平面湍流槽道**: LES验证
2. **跨音速压气机叶栅**: Wall-resolved LES
   - 激波-湍流边界层相互作用(SWBLI)
   - 高保真捕捉复杂流动结构

## 性能分析
- Strong and weak scaling evaluation
- AMD MI300A compute partitioning modes影响
- 跨音速压缩机级cascade的 demanding flow problems
