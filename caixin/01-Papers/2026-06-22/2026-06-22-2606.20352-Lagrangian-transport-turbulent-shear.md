# Planar Lagrangian Transport and Scalar-Gradient Organization in a Turbulent Reacting Shear Layer

## 论文信息
- **arXiv**: [2606.20352](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20352)
- **作者**: Sriram K. et al.
- **日期**: 2026-06-18
- **分类**: physics.flu-dyn
- **期刊**: Submitted to Chaos (20 pages, 23 figures)

## 核心贡献
结合FTLE、LCS等Lagrangian诊断工具，分析可压缩反应氢-空气混合层中的输运骨架与标量梯度组织。

## 物理问题
- **流动类型**: Supersonic, reacting hydrogen-air temporal mixing layer
- **模拟**: 3D DNS with time-resolved mid-plane data

## 分析工具箱
1. **Forward/Backward FTLE**: Finite-time Lyapunov exponent fields
2. **Operational FTLE-ridge skeletons**: LCS提取
3. **Cauchy-Green deformation measures**: 应变分析
4. **Shear-LCS metrics**: 剪切LCS度量
5. **Hyperbolic geodesic LCS**: 变分LCS提取

## 关键发现

### 1. 输运骨架
- 识别repelling和attracting FTLE ridges
- 量化ridge几何、占据、持久性
- 标量条件输运统计

### 2. Geodesic LCS
- 从Cauchy-Green张量重建
- 从high-λmax法向极大值的strainlines种子提取
- variational counterpart to operational ridge skeleton

### 3. 标量响应
- 与温度、混合分数、反应中间产物关联
- Forward/backward ridge overlap
- 强标量梯度富集
- Scalar-response lags remains compact
