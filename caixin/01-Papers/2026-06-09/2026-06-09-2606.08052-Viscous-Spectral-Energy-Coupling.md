# 2606.08052 — Viscous Spectral Energy Coupling Across Scales in Generalized Newtonian Fluids

## Metadata
- **arXiv**: [2606.08052](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.08052) [physics.flu-dyn]
- **Submitted**: 8 Jun 2026
- **Subjects**: Fluid Dynamics (physics.flu-dyn)

## Research Problem
Spectral energy dynamics of turbulent flows with **variable viscosity** using direct numerical simulation of **homogeneous isotropic turbulence** of generalized Newtonian fluids (Carreau constitutive model).

## Core Discovery
### Variable Viscosity Effect on Navier-Stokes
In spectral space, the viscous term becomes **nonlinear** and gives rise to a **convolution product**:
- Formally analogous to the convective term
- NOT purely local dissipation (unlike constant viscosity case)
- Carries both **conservative (transfer)** and **non-conservative (dissipation)** contributions

## Novel Computation
Viscous spectral coupling $\hat{V}(\mathbf{k}, \mathbf{k}')$ — does **NOT** satisfy detailed conservation property analogous to convective term.

### Two Distinct Spectral Regions Revealed:

1. **Sign-definite non-conservative region** near $\mathbf{k}' \approx \mathbf{0}$
2. **Transfer-like dipole** near $\mathbf{k}' \approx \mathbf{k}$ in **shear-thickening fluids**
   - Satisfies approximate antisymmetry: $\hat{V}(\mathbf{k}, \mathbf{k}') \approx -\hat{V}(\mathbf{k}', \mathbf{k})$
   - Signature of **conservative energy transfer**

## Paradigm Shift
**Energy transfer across scales** — traditionally attributed exclusively to convective nonlinearity — **can arise from ANY nonlinear term** in momentum equation.

## Energy Cascade Mechanism
- Viscous energy transfer participates in **forward cascade**
- Takes over convective transfer in **dissipation range**
- Connected to **power-law spectral decay** (replacing exponential cutoff in shear-thickening fluids)

## Feasibility Analysis: Viscous Spectral Energy Coupling

## 控制方程
Variable viscosity Navier-Stokes with Carreau model (generalized Newtonian)

## 数值方法
- DNS of homogeneous isotropic turbulence
- Spectral analysis of viscous term
- Convolution product analysis

## 关键发现
- 粘性项的卷积结构产生能量转移
- 剪切增稠流体中的 dipole 效应
- 替代对流非线性实现尺度间能量传输

- 谱计算成本中等
