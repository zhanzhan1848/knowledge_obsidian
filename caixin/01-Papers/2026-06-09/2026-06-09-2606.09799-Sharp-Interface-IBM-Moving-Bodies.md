# 2606.09799 — Sharp-Interface Immersed Boundary Method for Moving Bodies

## Metadata

- **Author**: Giovanni Vagnoli
- **Submitted**: 8 Jun 2026
- **Subjects**: Fluid Dynamics (physics.flu-dyn)

## Research Problem
Simulating flows around complex geometries and moving bodies — trade-off between precision and computational efficiency in immersed boundary methods (IBMs).

## Core Innovation
Novel **sharp-interface IBM** for incompressible flows involving:
- Moving, deformable, arbitrary-thickness bodies
- Fast tagging algorithm
- Two-sided Eulerian forcing strategy
- **Consistent mass correction** reducing splitting error of fractional-step schemes
- Preserves discrete Laplacian operator structure
- Retains efficiency of direct Poisson solvers (avoids cut-cell, multigrid, projection overhead)

## Key Methods
- **Sharp-interface enforcement** of no-slip condition
- **Second-order accuracy** in boundary enforcement
- Small transpiration errors
- Naturally handles moving boundaries

## Numerical Approach
- Fractional-step schemes with mass correction
- Direct Poisson solvers (efficient, no overhead)
- Two-sided forcing vs. cut-cell approaches

## Applications Demonstrated
1. Rigid body flows
2. Deformable body flows
3. Turbulent flows
4. Biologically-inspired flows

## Feasibility Analysis: Sharp-Interface IBM for Moving Bodies

## 控制方程
Incompressible Navier-Stokes equations with immersed boundary forcing

## 数值方法
- 离散化：Sharp-interface IBM with consistent mass correction
- 求解器：Direct Poisson solvers (efficient)
- 稳定性：Second-order accurate no-slip enforcement

## 计算成本
- 避免 cut-cell/multigrid 开销
- Fast tagging algorithm
- 适合大规模并行

## 推荐结论
✅ 推荐实现 — 精度与效率兼得，适合复杂几何和动边界问题
