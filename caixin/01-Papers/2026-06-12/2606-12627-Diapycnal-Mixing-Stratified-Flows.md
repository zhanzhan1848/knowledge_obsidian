# Two Pathways to Diapycnal Mixing in Strongly Stratified Flows with No Initial Vertical Shear

- **arXiv**: [2606.12627](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12627)
- **Date**: Thu, 11 Jun 2026
- **Subjects**: physics.flu-dyn; astro-ph.SR

## 核心创新点
- 水平剪切不稳定在强分层（low Froude number）流动中即使在 large mean Richardson number 时也活跃
- 结合 linear theory 和 DNS 研究两种湍流路径
- 两种路径激发不同的 vertical scales，导致不同的 peak mixing efficiencies

## 两种路径

### Pathway 1
- Primary horizontal shear instability → vertically-modulated eigenmodes → vertical shear emerges
- → Secondary small-scale KH instabilities on buoyancy scale (at large Re_b)

### Pathway 2
- Vertically-invariant eigenmode initially dominates
- → Nonlinear evolution → long-lived 2D columnar vortical flow
- → Secondary 3D hyperbolic instabilities → vertical shear emerges
- → Tertiary small-scale KH instabilities

## 关键结论
- Vertical shear + small-scale KH instabilities 是 horizontal shear instabilities 在强分层流动中的不可避免副产品
- 两种路径激发不同的 vertical scales → 不同的 mixing efficiency
