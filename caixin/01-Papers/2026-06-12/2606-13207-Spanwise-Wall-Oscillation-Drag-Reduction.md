# Duty-Cycle Modulation of the Self-Sustaining Process by Spanwise Wall Oscillation

- **arXiv**: [2606.13207](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13207)
- **Date**: Thu, 11 Jun 2026
- **Authors**: Lionel Agostini Dr
- **Subjects**: physics.flu-dyn

## 核心创新点
- DNS 研究 turbulent channel flow（Re_τ ≈ 200）with spanwise wall actuation
- Quasi-square-wave waveform（ impulsively transitions + constant-velocity plateaus）
- Phase-resolved analysis 揭示 SSP (self-sustaining process) 机制

## 关键发现
- **Reversal Phase**: Stokes strain passes through zero, SSP resumes, streaks regenerate
- **Displacement Phase**: Sustained Stokes strain diverts wall-normal vorticity spanwise via vortex tilting, depletes SSP precursors, suppresses streaks
- Quasi-square wave 比 optimal sinusoidal 多 2.5% gross drag reduction
- Stochastic enstrophy-budget analysis 确认机制

## 控制方程
- Navier-Stokes equations
- Channel flow boundary conditions
- SSP framework (self-sustaining process)

## 数值方法
- **方法**: DNS (Direct Numerical Simulation)
- **Re_τ**: ≈ 200
- **Actuation**: Spanwise wall oscillation, quasi-square wave

## 结论
Duty-cycle switching 直接揭示了 drag reduction 的因果链。
