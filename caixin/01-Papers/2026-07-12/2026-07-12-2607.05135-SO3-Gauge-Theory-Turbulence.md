# 2607.05135: An SO(3) Gauge Theory of Turbulence with Spontaneous Symmetry Breaking

## Metadata

## Abstract
Shows that fully developed isotropic turbulence exhibits dual nature (continuous energy cascade + discrete vortex filaments) arising from spontaneously broken SO(3) gauge symmetry. Identifies angular momentum L = r × u as non-Abelian gauge connection and radial velocity as Higgs field. Turbulent vacuum described by SO(3) Georgi-Glashow model.

## Key Findings
- SO(3) gauge symmetry breaking explains turbulence duality
- Energy spectra obey strict 1:2 equipartition over inertial range
- Mass gap MW ≈ 40 partitions energy into massless U(1) sector and massive SO(3)/U(1) sector
- "Worms" identified as macroscopic 't Hooft-Polyakov monopoles
- Wilson loop exhibits clean area law confirming confining vacuum
