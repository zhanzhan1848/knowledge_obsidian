# 2607.07443: A fully one-sided diffuse-interface immersed boundary method for wall-modeled large-eddy simulation

## Metadata
- **arXiv**: [2607.07443](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07443)
- **Authors**: Qian Mao
- **Submitted**: Wed, 8 Jul 2026
- **Category**: physics.flu-dyn

## Abstract
Develops WMLES approach based on fully one-sided diffuse-interface immersed boundary method (FODIBM). Removes cross-boundary diffusion effect by performing interpolation and spreading exclusively inside immersed body. Wall-shear-stress enforcement via coupling with explicit wall model. Tau-model preserves total shear-stress balance below reference height.

## Key Innovations
- Fully one-sided DIBM (FODIBM) removes cross-boundary diffusion
- Wall-shear-stress enforcement with explicit wall model
- Tau-model based on modeled turbulent shear-stress tensor
- Validated in high-Reynolds turbulent channel flows
- Good agreement with DNS for mean velocity, Reynolds stress, skin-friction

## Numerical Method
- Diffuse-interface immersed boundary method (DIBM)
- Wall-modeled large-eddy simulation (WMLES)
- Explicit wall model
- Turbulent channel flow, NACA23012 airfoil
