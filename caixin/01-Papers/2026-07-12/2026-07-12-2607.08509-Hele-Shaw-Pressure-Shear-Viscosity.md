# 2607.08509: Hele-Shaw Flow With Pressure and Shear Rate Dependent Viscosity

## Abstract
Investigates behavior of fluid with viscosity depending simultaneously on pressure and shear rate within a Hele-Shaw cell featuring sharp corner geometry. Extends previous analyses on purely pressure-dependent and yield-stress fluids. Derives modified flow equations adapted to Hele-Shaw flow for generalized Newtonian fluid. Particular solution obtained near corner region with numerical simulations validating theoretical results.

## Key Findings
- Viscosity simultaneously depends on pressure and shear rate
- New perspective on complex fluid behavior in narrow-gap geometries around sharp edge
- Modified Hele-Shaw flow equations for generalized Newtonian fluid
- Applications: biomedical devices, thin film technologies, injection molding

## Control Equations
