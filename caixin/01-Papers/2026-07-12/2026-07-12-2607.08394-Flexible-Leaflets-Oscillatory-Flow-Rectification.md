# 2607.08394: On the rectification of oscillatory flows by flexible leaflets in a confined geometry

## Metadata
- **arXiv**: [2607.08394](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08394)
- **Authors**: Martin Brandenbourger
- **Submitted**: Thu, 9 Jul 2026
- **Category**: physics.flu-dyn; cond-mat.soft

## Abstract
Investigates numerically the collective interaction of multiple asymmetric leaflets within a channel at low Reynolds number. Uses symmetrically oscillating plates to isolate system from background asymmetries. Develops analytical framework for transport evaluation, extended to time-dependent channel oscillations.

## Key Findings
- High leaflet densities maximize collective interactions and net transport
- Elastoviscous number (comparing viscous hydrodynamic forces to restorative elastic forces) has optimal value maximizing net flow
- Framework for analyzing collective slender structures in viscous environments
- Applications: biological fluid transport, microfluidic design

## Control Parameters
- Reynolds number (low)
