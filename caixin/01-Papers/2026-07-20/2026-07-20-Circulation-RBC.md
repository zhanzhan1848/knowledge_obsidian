## Keywords
circulation statistics, Rayleigh-Bénard convection, turbulence, vortex, area rule, thermal boundary layer

## Research Question
Do velocity circulation properties discovered in HIT persist in other turbulent flows?

## Key Findings
1. **DNS of Rayleigh-Bénard convection** at Ra=10⁹, Pr=O(1)
2. Within thermal boundary layer (TBL):
   - Elementary vortex distribution strongly correlated with temperature field
   - Core aspect ratio statistics significantly altered
3. **PDFs of circulation** for planar contours parallel to walls show features akin to HIT
4. *Area Rule* (connection between circulation and minimal surfaces) **well satisfied** except transitional region
5. Away from TBL: behavior resembles HIT
   - Intermittent spatial distribution of vortex tubes determined by dissipation field

## Turbulence Physics
- Small-scale structures in circulation fluctuations
- Link between vortex tubes and energy dissipation
- Scaling laws in convective turbulence

## Significance
- Extends HIT circulation statistics to convective turbulence
- Shows universality of Area Rule across turbulent flows
- Thermal boundary layer modifies but doesn't eliminate vortex structures
