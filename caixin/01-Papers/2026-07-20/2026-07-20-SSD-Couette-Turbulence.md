## Keywords
Couette turbulence, statistical state dynamics, roll streak structures, Navier-Stokes, self-sustaining process

## Core Problem
Wide channel Couette (WCC) turbulence consists of steady roll-streak structures (RSS), but N-S equations in velocity variables admit no linear RSS instability or stable equilibrium.

## Key Findings
1. In **second-order statistical state dynamics (SSD)**:
   - Fixed-point state arises from **modal RSS instability**
   - Equilibrates as exact, attracting RSS at spanwise wavenumber 3
2. Fixed point comprises:
   - Rank-1 streamwise mean flow
   - Conjugate pair of neutral eigenmodes
   - Rank-2 fluctuation covariance
3. Regularized by **roll advection** rather than viscosity
4. WCC turbulence = **spanwise tiling** by fixed-point RSS unit cell

## Physical Mechanism
- Modal instability of RSS in SSD framework
- Self-sustaining process without linear instability in physical space
- Tiling by unit cells explains turbulent structure

## Significance
- Provides **analytical basis** for Couette turbulence
- Links SSD theory to Navier-Stokes turbulence structure
