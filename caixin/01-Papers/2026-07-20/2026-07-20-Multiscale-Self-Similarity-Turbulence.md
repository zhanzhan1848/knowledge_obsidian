# Data-driven Identification of Multiscale Self-Similarity in Turbulent Flows

## Keywords
self-similarity, turbulence, turbulent channel flow, decaying turbulence, scaling laws, data-driven, dissipation law

## Core Innovation
Algorithm for **systematic identification of multiscale self-similarity** from data without prior knowledge of governing equations.

## Key Findings
1. **Inner and outer self-similarity** successfully identified in:
   - Turbulent channel flow
   - Late-stage homogeneous decaying turbulence
2. **Intermediate region** reveals similarity expressions independent of inner/outer scales
3. Recovers:
   - **Logarithmic law** in turbulent channel flow
   - **-5/3 law** in late-stage decaying turbulence
4. Discovers **Barenblatt corrections** to scaling laws
5. Applied to early-stage decaying turbulence with nonclassical dissipation law

## Methodology
- Data-driven framework (Bempedelis et al. 2025)
- No prior knowledge of governing equations required
- Systematic multiscale analysis

## Turbulence Physics
- Classical dissipation laws in late-stage decay
- Nonclassical dissipation law in early-stage decay
- Barenblatt power-law scaling corrections
- Dual cascade behavior

## Significance
- Novel data-driven route to scaling laws
- Framework for extracting similarity from DNS/experimental data
- Connections to turbulence closure modeling
