# 2606.14287: Surface-tension calibration for N-phase mixtures

## 基本信息
- **arXiv ID**: [2606.14287](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.14287)
- **作者**: Marco ten Eikelder et al.
- **日期**: 2026-06-12
- **主题**: Diffuse-interface, Cahn-Hilliard, N-phase Navier-Stokes, surface tension calibration

## 背景问题
- Diffuse-interface (phase-field) 模型广泛用于复杂流体界面动力学
- 多相模型校准困难：多个pairwise surface tensions需同时匹配
- 平衡路径受Gibbs simplex约束

## 方法
- **核心**: Thermodynamically consistent calibration for N-phase Cahn-Hilliard free energies
- **关键**: 确定 symmetric capillary matrix 匹配目标surface tensions
- **创新**: Rescaling strategy 调整diffuse interface widths至mesh-resolvable值，同时保持calibrated surface tensions

## 应用
- N-phase Navier-Stokes-Cahn-Hilliard flows
- 通用框架适用于任意N

## 创新点
1. 通过equilibrium profiles隐式确定surface tensions和diffuse thicknesses
2. 解决Gibbs simplex约束下的多相标定问题
3. 宽度rescaling保持表面张力不变

🥢 可行性分析：N-phase diffuse-interface calibration

- Cahn-Hilliard方程：$\partial_t \phi = \nabla \cdot (M \nabla \mu)$
- 化学势：$\mu = f'(\phi) - \epsilon^2 \nabla^2 \phi$
