# 2606.13969: Numerical simulations of transition and long-term response of a wind turbine airfoil

## 基本信息
- **arXiv ID**: [2606.13969](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13969)
- **作者**: Thales Coelho Leite Fava et al.
- **日期**: 2026-06-11
- **主题**: LES wind turbine airfoil, wall-resolved LES

## 方法
- **求解器**: Nek5000, EllipSys
- **方法**: Wall-resolved large-eddy simulation (LES)
- **几何**: FFA-W3 wind-turbine airfoil (DTU 10-MW Reference Wind Turbine)
- **Re**: $Re_c = 1 \times 10^5$, $AoA = 3.1^\circ - 3.3^\circ$
- **域**: spanwise width 10% chord

## 关键结果
1. **LES验证**: EllipSys与Nek5000结果close agreement
2. **边界层**: EllipSys underpredicts TS waves due to higher numerical dissipation
3. **KH波**: Correctly predicts Kelvin-Helmholtz mode in laminar separation bubble
4. **低频振荡**: Normal-force coefficient modulation (10.5% amplitude, period 48 flow-through times)
5. **Strouhal数**: $St = 0.0012$ (lower than previously observed)
6. **SPOD**: Extracts KH wavepacket mode shape

## 创新点
- 使用 spectral proper orthogonal decomposition (SPOD) 提取KH wavepacket
- 发现 periodic stalling/reattachment 触发振荡机制
- 提出 absolute instability 条件（reverse flow on both sides）

## 公式/方法细节
- $f^* = f c / U_\infty = 0.021$
- $St = f \sin(AoA) = 0.0012$
- Kelvin-Helmholtz instability in laminar separation bubble
- PSE (parabolized stability equation) for validation

## 可行性分析
🥢 可行性分析：LES风 turbine airfoil transition

## 控制方程
- Navier-Stokes (incompressible, wall-resolved LES)
- Continuity: $\nabla \cdot \mathbf{u} = 0$

## 数值方法
- 离散化：Spectral h/p elements (Nektar++), finite volume (EllipSys)
- 求解器：Nek5000 (spectral element), EllipSys (finite volume)
- 稳定性：CFL约束，时间步长受涡结构演化限制

## 计算成本
- 网格：spanwise domain width = 0.1c (足够捕捉主要扰动)
- 并行性：Nek5000 高度可扩展

## 推荐结论
✅ 推荐实现 (LES风 turbine应用)
