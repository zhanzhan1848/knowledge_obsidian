## Core Innovation
Quantum algorithm for PIV (QuPIV):
- **Quantum Fourier Transforms** for cross-correlation
- Multidimensional state preparation
- Contracted ground-state projector for amplitude amplification
- Significant gate reduction in quantum circuit

## Classical vs Quantum PIV
- Classical PIV: FFT-based cross-correlation for velocity vectors
- Quantum QuPIV: Quantum Fourier Transform for potentially exponential speedup
