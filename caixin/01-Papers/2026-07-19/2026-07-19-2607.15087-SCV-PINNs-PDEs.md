# 2607.15087 - Split Complex-Valued PINNs for Forward and Inverse PDEs

## Metadata
- **arXiv**: [2607.15087](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15087)
- **Subjects**: Fluid Dynamics (physics.flu-dyn)
- **Date**: Thu, 16 Jul 2026
- **Comments**: 54 pages, 31 figures, 12 tables

## Core Innovation
Proposes Split Complex-Valued Physics-Informed Neural Networks (SCV-PINN):
- Complex-valued network parameters and activations
- Split activation functions (real + imaginary components)
- Simultaneous amplitude and phase learning

## Key Benchmarks (Fluid-related)
- **Kovasznay flow** (Re = 20)
- **Lid-driven cavity** (Re = 100)
- **Inverse Navier-Stokes** (Re = 100)
- **3D Navier-Stokes Beltrami flow** (relative L2 error: 4.07×10⁻⁵)

## Numerical Results
| Benchmark | SCV-PINN Error | vs RV-PINN |
|-----------|----------------|------------|
| Kovasznay | 4.07×10⁻⁵ | Better |
| Beltrami 3D | 4.07×10⁻⁵ | Consistently lower |

## Advantages
- Mitigates spectral bias
- Enhanced expressivity for oscillatory dynamics
- Better parameter identification in inverse problems

## Significance for CFD
✅ Strong potential for inverse flow problems, turbulence modeling
