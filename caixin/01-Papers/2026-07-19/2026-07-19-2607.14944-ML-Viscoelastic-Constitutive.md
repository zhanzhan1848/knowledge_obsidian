## Core Innovation
Reduced-dimensional Tensor Basis Neural Network (TBNN) + Universal Differential Equations (UDE):
- Frame-invariant, nonlinear viscoelastic constitutive models
- **Reduced basis formulation** for physical consistency + numerical stability
- Training on oscillatory shear flows only

## Architecture
- **UDE architecture** embedded in finite volume solver
- Constitutive response generated dynamically at runtime
- TBNN for tensor basis representation

## Benchmarks
- 4:1 sudden contraction (2D/3D)
- Cross-slot flow
- Viscoelastic extensional flows

## Key Results
- Captures flow-induced elastic instabilities
- Extrapolation from shear → extensional flows
- First normal stress difference improves accuracy at high Deborah numbers

## Significance for CFD
✅ Strong relevance for complex fluids, rheology, polymer flows
