### FA-QEM: Fast and Robust Mesh Simplification

**核心创新**: Feature-Aware Quadric Error Metric (FA-QEM)

**多目标二次误差公式**:
- 几何偏差 (Geometric Deviation)
- 边界曲率 (Boundary Curvature)

**实验结果**:
- 更低几何误差
- 更好视觉保真度
- substantially faster runtimes
- CVPRW 2026 3D4S Best Paper Award Runner-up
